About

Welcome to the Mu Alpha Theta website for Lawton Chiles. Currently, we are the third ranked club in the nation! This is a mathematics honor society for high school students competing in competitions while also strivng to help the community through events and fundraisers. Competitive members can go to regional, state and national competitions while non-competitive members can help tutor students, join relay for life, and even sell pies for a pie day fundraiser.
